About us

Paws on the Move began in 2010 as a small dog walking service in a quiet neighborhood. The founder, a lifelong animal lover, noticed that many pet owners were busy with work and didn’t always have time to walk their dogs. Understanding how important regular walks are for a dog’s health and happiness, they started a service to help both dogs and their owners. At first, Paws on the Move was just a one-person operation. The founder would visit clients' homes and take their dogs for walks, giving each dog personal attention. These walks weren’t just about exercise—they were about fun, care, and companionship. Dogs were happier, and owners appreciated the dependable service. As word spread, the company grew. The founder hired a small team of dog walkers who shared the same love for animals and high standards of care. They got to know each dog’s needs, favorite routes, and personalities. This personalized approach made Paws on the Move stand out. Eventually, the company expanded its services to include pet sitting, group walks, and basic training support. It also built strong ties with the local community, supporting shelters and attending pet events. Despite the growth, the company stayed focused on its mission: to offer kind, reliable care that helps dogs stay active and loved. Today, Paws on the Move is a well-known and trusted part of the community. It serves many happy clients while holding onto its original values—treating every dog like family and making sure they get the attention, exercise, and joy they deserve.
